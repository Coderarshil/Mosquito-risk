🛡️ Protection Guide
🧴 Chemical & Personal
✓
DEET ≥30% repellent — Apply on exposed skin, reapply every 4–6 hours. Essential during peak hours.
✓
Protective clothing — Light-colored long sleeves and pants during dawn and dusk activities.
✓
Pyrethroid-treated nets — Use insecticide-treated bed nets, especially in malaria-prone zones.
🪣 Larval Source Reduction
✓
Remove stagnant water weekly — Flower pots, old tyres, AC trays. Larvae mature in 7–10 days.
✓
Cover water storage containers — Tight-fitting lids prevent oviposition and sunlight evaporation.
✓
Window screens & door seals — Fine mesh (18×14) prevents indoor entry; check for tears regularly.
🐟 Biological Control
✓
Bti (Bacillus thuringiensis) — Apply BTI granules to standing water that cannot be drained.
✓
Gambusia fish in ponds — These larvivorous fish consume up to 100 larvae per day in open water bodies.
